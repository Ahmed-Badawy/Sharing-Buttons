Sharing Buttons

The best sharing plugin on the internet. Now you can share Every thing every where. It provides API to:
[ Facebook, Facebook's Dialog API, Facebook's old sharer.php API, Twitter, Google-Plus, Linkedin, Pinterest, Stumbleupon, VK, Xing, Tumblr, Both Reddit (text,link) API's, Buffer, Digg, Plurk, Delicious, Hackernews, Instapaper, Pocket, Flipboard, Weibo, Renren, Myspace, Google's Blogger, Baidu ]
Also can send Email, Print & Bookmark pages. Also Allow mobile sharing to apps like: [ Whats APP, Google-Maps, Phone Call, Internet Call, Skype Adding & Calling, Viper adding & calling, Sending over Telegram, Also Line APP] You Can even generate QR & Barcode from URLs to send them to friends.
